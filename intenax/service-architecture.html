  • What does an architect do?
    An architect designs buildings, focusing on both appearance and functionality. They create plans and blueprints, work with clients to understand their needs, and collaborate with engineers and contractors to ensure safety, compliance, and quality. Architects may also oversee construction to ensure the project aligns with their design.
  • How do I choose the right architecture company?
    To choose the right architecture company, find one with experience in your project type, a style that matches your vision, and a good reputation. Ensure they communicate well, respect budgets and timelines, and embrace sustainable, modern practices. These qualities will help you select a firm that fits your needs.
